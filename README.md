# Deck : Twelve Labours
Deck Twelve Labours is a 2D action card battle game inspired by the myth of Hercules and the Twelve Labours.
Players explore different areas, defeat monsters, and use cards and items strategically to overcome various challenges.

# Gameplay

Move through the world and encounter enemies.

Use cards during battles to attack or gain advantages.

Collect and use items to assist in combat.

Progress through different labours inspired by Greek mythology.

# Controls

WASD – Move

Space – Skip dialogue / UI

I – Open inventory

Drag Card – Use card in battle

ESC – Open menu
